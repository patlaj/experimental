  2. Choose how you'll register and sign-in

    How do you want to sign in every time you use your account?

    Option 1: With a new or existing GCKey username and password

    Register with GCKey

    Sign up for a new GCKey username and password to use with your account, or sign in to register with your existing GCKey username and password.

    Option 2: With your device instead of a password

    Register your device

    Instead of a username and password, register with the finger print, face or a passkey that you use to unlock your mobile device or computer.

    Sign in without a username and password

    When you register and then sign-in, you can approve it with the same passkey you use to unlock your device, such as:

    • your finger print
    • your face
    • a Personal Identification Number (PIN)
    • a button on a security key you plug in

    Next time you sign in, use the same device that you used when you registered. If your passkey, fingerprint or PIN is shared across devices, you may also be able to use a different device.

    If the device you're using doesn't support passkeys, click the back button and choose a different option to register.

    Option 3: Through your online banking credentials

    Register through an Interac® Sign-in Partner

    You must have an online account already with one of the Sign-in Partners.

    Your banking ID and password are never shared with the Government of Canada and your account details are never shared with your financial institution.

    View list of Sign-In Partners [DISCUSS THIS]

    Option 4: Through your province

    Register with a provincial ID

    BC Services Card and MyAlberta Digital ID are available.

    You must have an account with the province's website to register this way.

    Learn more about provincial partners
